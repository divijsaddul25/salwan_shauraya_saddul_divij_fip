ZIMA Citrus Flavoured

ZIMA Citrus Flavoured: A tantalizing blend of tangy lemon, lime, and hints of orange. Delicately balanced for a crisp, clean taste with a refreshing citrus kick. Enjoy the bright, effervescent flavors in every sip. Perfect for those who crave a taste of summer all year round.

ZIMA Raspberry Flavoured

ZIMA Raspberry Flavoured offers a delightful fusion of sweet and tart flavors in every sip. Bursting with the succulent essence of ripe raspberries, this beverage captivates the palate with its vibrant taste and refreshing finish. Its crisp and clear profile provides the perfect canvas for the bold raspberry notes to shine, creating a harmonious balance of sweetness and tanginess. Whether enjoyed on its own or as a mixer in cocktails, ZIMA Raspberry Flavoured is sure to elevate any gathering with its irresistible fruity allure.

ZIMA Herb Flavoured

ZIMA Herb Flavoured presents a unique and sophisticated twist on refreshment. Infused with a carefully crafted blend of aromatic herbs, this beverage offers a refreshing and intriguing taste experience. Each sip unfolds with subtle hints of basil, mint, and thyme, perfectly balanced to awaken the senses without overwhelming the palate. The herbal infusion adds depth and complexity, making it an ideal choice for those seeking a more refined alternative to traditional drinks. Whether enjoyed on a sunny afternoon or as a sophisticated mixer in cocktails, ZIMA Herb Flavoured promises to elevate any occasion with its distinctively refreshing taste.
