ABOUT US

Our Journey: Crafting Nature s Beer

Zima, originating in the heart of Eastern Europe, derives its essence from the diverse Slavic heritage, mirroring the intricate layers of the beloved Matryoshka doll. Our journey commenced with Ivan Petrov, a skilled craftsman captivated by the heartwarming customs of winter gatherings. Drawing inspiration from generations of Eastern European recipes, Ivan painstakingly blended carefully selected herbs, spices, and fruits to craft Zima—a beverage that embodies the essence of the season. Starting from its modest beginnings in Ivan's workshop, Zima has grown into a cherished emblem of Eastern European hospitality. At Zima, we pay homage to our heritage by sourcing ingredients locally, ensuring that each bottle reflects the authenticity of our homeland. Embrace the warmth of tradition and the invigorating chill of winter with every sip of Zima—a testament to the timeless allure of Eastern Europe.
