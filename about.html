ABOUT US

Our Story

Savoring Nature's Beer

ZIMA's tale unfolds in the vibrant cultural landscape of 1990, where it emerged as a pioneering beverage brand deeply rooted in Slavic heritage. Originating amidst the traditions of the Slavic people, ZIMA swiftly became synonymous with innovation and tradition. Our journey commenced with our visionary founder, Ivan Petrovich, a skilled bartender who meticulously crafted his own unique blends of fruit-infused beverages. Roaming from village to village, Ivan's creations delighted the taste buds of locals, sparking an insatiable demand for his concoctions. To meet this growing fervor, he established a modest stall, marking the inception of ZIMA. Over the years, our brand has thrived, grounded in the authenticity of our Slavic heritage. Today, ZIMA proudly honors its roots by sourcing the finest ingredients from Slavic lands, ensuring that each sip embodies the essence of our rich cultural legacy.

Squeezing Freshness In Every Can

Our beverages are crafted with a blend of expertise and unwavering dedication, driven by our passion to deliver an unparalleled drinking experience. We are committed to continuous innovation, ensuring that each sip exceeds your expectations. Customer satisfaction is at the core of everything we do, guiding us to consistently strive for excellence.
